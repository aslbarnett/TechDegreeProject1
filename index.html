Alexandra Barnett

Martial Artist

About

Alex has been training in martial arts for the past 17 years. She began training in Kickboxing in 1999 in Essex at The Fitness Academy under Guro Laurence Sandum. Since then, she has expanded her knowledge by training in a variety of martial arts under some of the world's best including Sifu Francis Fong, Guro Dan Inosanto and Guro Daniel Lonero.

Alex is available for private tuition or small group tuition. Please contact for more details.
